Director | Etnography

Gharisa Nur Alam

Hello, I'm Ica, a director with a deep interest in culture and ethnography. My works often explore indigenous communities, traditional knowledge, and local narratives—bringing visibility to underrepresented voices. With a background in both fiction and documentary filmmaking, I’ve directed and produced a range of films centered on cultural identity, including research-driven projects such as Selamat Tinggal Toean-Toean, produced in the Netherlands with support from Indonesiana, investigating the story of Maluku and Thomas Matulessy (Kapitan Pattimura).
Through cinematographic storytelling, I strive to preserve cultural memory and question colonial legacies. My approach is grounded in field research and visual sensitivity—bridging anthropology and cinema. Let’s tell stories that remember, resist, and restore.
